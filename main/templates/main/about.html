About DSST

Our story, vision, mission, and the values that guide us.

Est. 1985

A Legacy of Excellence in Education

DSST was founded in 1985 with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide quality education that nurtures every child's potential. Over the decades, we have grown from a small school of 100 students to a vibrant institution of over 2,500 learners across Nursery to Class XII.

We believe education goes beyond textbooks. Our holistic approach combines rigorous academics with sports, arts, and real-world skills.
